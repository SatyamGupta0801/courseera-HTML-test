Chole Bhature

Chole bhature (Hindi: छोले भटूरे) is a food dish originating from northern India. It is a combination of chana masala (spicy white chickpeas) and bhatura, a fried bread made from maida. There is a distinct Punjabi variant of the dish. Chhole bhature is often eaten as a breakfast dish, sometimes accompanied with lassi.
It can also be street food or a complete meal and may be accompanied with onions, pickled carrots, green chutney or achaar
